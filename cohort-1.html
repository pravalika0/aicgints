The first cohort of Envisage Pre-incubation Program was kickstarted with 16 ideas from G.Narayanamma Institute of Technology and Science (For Women) in the presence of British Deputy High Commissioner His Excellency Gareth Wynn Owen.

The students pitched their innovative ideas in front of the British Deputy High Commissioner on 2nd Dec 2022. The first cohort successfully ended on 21st August 2023 with market-viable proof of concepts.

Graduated Teams

Stress Analyzer for Women

Stress management tool that Motivates women by sending positive messages if any kind of stress is Detected in women.

Two wheeler Safety

To keep the two-wheelers drivers safe, with Movable backrest and the device for monitoring the abnormality of two wheeler driver's health

Reduce carbon emissions

Use a novel organometallic compound that converts CO2 to O2

Retina capturing

Retina capturing using portable device and Classification of images for prevalence of eye disease

Biodegradable bags

Manufacturing of biodegradable bags for sanititary/menstrual waste management

Smart Bag for Women Safety

Weed eradicating automation
