Envisage Pre-incubation

  • Envisage Pre-incubation Program is a 3 months Program to nurture young women's minds to come up with an entrepreneurial mindset and to develop a market prototype.
  • The aspiring entrepreneurs - students / startups will be given in-depth training on entrepreneurship and innovation starting from idea validation to creating business plans.

Who can apply?

  • Women aspiring to become entrepreneurs
  • Women having an idea
  • Women have a prototype but does not know how to take it to the market

What does the Program cover?

  • What does the Program cover?
  • What does the Program cover?
  • Product Market Fit
  • Business Model Canvas
  • Understanding of IP Rights and filing the IP
  • Development of a working prototype
